Privacy Policy – Translation Bot

The Translation Bot for Discord is designed solely for the purpose of automatically translating messages.

What data is processed?

  • Message content from specific Discord channels
  • Username (display only, not stored)

What happens to the data?

The messages are sent securely to the DeepL API for translation and then discarded. No data is stored.

Who has access to the data?

Only authorized bot systems have access to messages in selected channels. No data is shared with third parties.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact the bot developer via Discord.
